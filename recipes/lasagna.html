Bitch Lasagna

Description

"Bitch Lasagna" is a quote taken from a viral Facebook Messenger screenshot which is often associated with the corrupted English words Bobs and Vegana.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegana juice
  • 3 tsp of complete open bob
  • A splash of berthole
  • 1 whole milk truck

Steps:
